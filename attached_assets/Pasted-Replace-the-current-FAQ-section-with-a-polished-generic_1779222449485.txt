FAQ items:

1. What does a chiropractor help with?
Chiropractic care focuses on how the spine, joints, muscles, and nervous system work together. Many patients visit a chiropractor for back pain, neck pain, stiffness, mobility concerns, or general wellness support.

2. What happens during a first visit?
Your first visit typically includes a conversation about your symptoms, health history, and goals. Dr. Smith may also evaluate movement, posture, and areas of discomfort before discussing a care plan.

3. Do I need to be in pain to see a chiropractor?
No. Some patients seek care because they are dealing with discomfort, while others want support with mobility, posture, or ongoing wellness.

4. How do I request an appointment?
You can request an appointment through the form on this website. The office will follow up to confirm availability and next steps.

5. Where is Echelon Chiropractic located?
Echelon Chiropractic is located inside VitaLive Health and Wellness at 301 N Main Street in Dickson, TN 37055.

6. Is online scheduling available?
Online scheduling is coming soon. For now, patients can request an appointment through the website or contact the office directly.

7. What services are offered?
Echelon Chiropractic offers chiropractic adjustments, new patient examinations and re-examinations, therapeutic exercises and rehabilitation, soft tissue therapy, wellness and maintenance care, and flexion distraction.

8. What should I bring to my first visit?
Bring a photo ID and any information that may help Dr. Smith better understand your health history, symptoms, and goals.
